ShakeBox is the next generation in convenient nutrition

ShakeBox is a shake-dispensing vending machine, preparing made-to-order health shakes conveniently at the relevant locations - gyms, studios and training areas. A free downloadable app will allow customers access to detailed nutritional information and partake in the loyalty programme. Shakes, broadly speaking, will conform to one of four categories - protein, pre-workout, meal replacements, and smoothies. ShakeBox allows affordable, convenient purchasing of quality gym-oriented shakes before, during, or after sessions.

Fast

ShakeBox can deliver a shake in under a minute. Convenience for our customers is a priority.

Powerful

ShakeBox sources South Africa's top nutrition brands, as well as sourcing fresh, local ingredients for our smoothies.

Connected

ShakeBox connects the user with a personalised app that allows them to reward themselves for frequent use and find out nutritional information of their purchase

ShakeBox is a unique and revolutionary product

Nothing quite like ShakeBox currently exists on the market. There are a few vending products that share certain similarities in terms of the technical design, but these products would need significant adaptations to meet the requirements of ShakeBox.

ShakeBox is about engineered excellence

The physical design appeals to its user base and draws their attention. Our product is visually attractive and encourages positive perceptions, building customer-machine trust.

ShakeBox will encourage user engagement via its app, which will track customer habits, use rewards to encourage repeat purchases, and increase the legitimacy of the products by sharing nutritional information, which will help build the ShakeBox brand.

Connecting the machines to the internet will allow them to be monitored remotely, so that employees may use real-time information to know when they need restocking. Further analysis can help ShakeBox determine the most appealing products and flavours
